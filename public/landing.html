How it works
Three steps to movie night

No accounts. No downloads. Just a link.

Step 01
Create a room

Pick tonight's vibe — thrilling, feel-good, emotional, or let us surprise you. Then share the link with your group.

Step 02
Everyone picks their favorites

Each person searches and picks 2–3 movies they love. No pressure, no judgment. Everyone also votes on their vibe.

Step 03
Get your match

WatchMatch analyzes everyone's taste, finds the overlap, and recommends the one movie that works for the whole group.
